League of Legends tournaments

League of Legends is one of the most popular multiplayer online battle arena games only rivaled by DOTA 2. Its competitive scene has grown exponentially since its release in 2009. With a variety of regional leagues and international events like the World Championship, LoL attracts millions of viewers worldwide.

The World Championship is the pinnacle and the top of competitive play in League of Legends. It features the best teams from around the world, competing for the prestigious title and a multi-million dollar prize pool. In addition to the World Championship, other notable tournaments include the Mid-Season Invitational (MSI), which is also a global tournament, the LCS (North America), LEC (Europe), LCK (Korea), and LPL (China).

World Championship

The biggest event in the League of Legends competitive scene, drawing teams from around the world to compete for the championship.

Mid-Season Invitational

An international event held between the Spring and Summer splits, featuring a limited number of the best teams from each region, competing for the MSI trophy.

Regional Leagues

Various regional leagues like LCS, LEC, LCK, and LPL are where the best teams compete throughout the year, aiming for a spot in the international events.
