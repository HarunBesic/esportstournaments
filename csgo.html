CSGO Majors and their history:

The CSGO Majors are more than just a competitive event; they are a testament to the passion and dedication of the Counter-Strike community. Since their inception in 2013, the Majors have become a stage where unforgettable stories are written. These tournaments attract millions of viewers worldwide, with fans tuning in to cheer for their favorite teams. Each Major features a unique atmosphere, shaped by the culture of the host city, the energy of the crowd, and the stakes on the line. Players pour countless hours into perfecting their skills, and teams strategize relentlessly to gain even the smallest edge over their rivals. From the underdog stories like Cloud9s victory at ELEAGUE Boston 2018 to the dominance of NAVI in Stockholm 2021, every Major has its own narrative, filled with emotion, drama, and incredible moments that leave a lasting legacy in esports history.

Best moments from some majors:

  1. DreamHack Winter 2013: Fnatic claimed the first-ever Major title in Jönköping, Sweden.
  2. ESL One Cologne 2015: One of the most iconic Majors, won by Fnatic.
  3. ELEAGUE Boston 2018: Cloud9 made history as the first North American team to win a Major.
  4. PGL Stockholm 2021: NAVI secured their first Major victory without dropping a single map.
  5. BLAST Paris Major 2023: The final CS:GO Major before the transition to Counter-Strike 2, won by Team Vitality.
